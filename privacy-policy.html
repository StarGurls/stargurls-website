At this stage, our Website is designed to provide general information and updates about StarGurls and our upcoming games. We only collect minimal and essential personal data, which may include:

Our Website uses only strictly necessary cookies. These cookies are essential for the Website to perform its basic functions, such as remembering your consent preferences on the cookie banner. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising cookies.

For more detailed information, please visit our Cookie Policy.
