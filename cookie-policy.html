Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to the Cookie Policy of StarGurls. This Policy explains how and why we use cookies and similar technologies on our website, https://www.stargurls.com.

We use cookies only to ensure our Website functions properly and securely.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device and remember certain information or preferences.

Cookies are generally categorized by their purpose:

  • Essential (Necessary) Cookies: Required for the basic site to function correctly and securely.
  • Performance/Analytics Cookies: Used to measure how visitors use the site and its performance (StarGurls does not use these).
  • Advertising/Targeting Cookies: Used for marketing and personalized ads (StarGurls does not use these).

2. How We Use Cookies

At StarGurls, we currently use only strictly essential cookies. These cookies help us:

  • Remember your cookie consent choice (so you don't see the banner again on every visit).
  • Help the website operate smoothly and securely (for example, managing basic session functionality).

Our use of these cookies is based on our legitimate interest to provide you with a functional and secure Website.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our Website, you may see a cookie banner. Because we only use essential cookies, the Website is designed to function with these enabled.

Essential cookies cannot be turned off via the Website because they are required for its basic operation.

However, you retain full control over all cookies via your web browser settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies manually using the controls provided by your specific browser.

Please note: If you choose to delete or block all cookies through your browser, some parts of the Website may not work correctly or may display errors.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not currently use third-party cookies for the operation of our Website. However, our Website contains links to external, third-party sites (such as our social media pages or retailers). If you click on these links and leave our Website, those third-party sites will use their own cookies and have their own Cookie Policies.

StarGurls is not responsible for the cookie practices or policies of any external websites. Please review their policies separately.

5. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time if we change the cookies we use or if legal requirements change. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised.
